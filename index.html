A safe, supportive space to heal and grow

Welcome. I’m Nicol Batra, an integrative therapist (MBACP) offering a gentle, inclusive space for you to explore, heal, and reconnect with yourself.

About Me

Hello, I’m Nicol – a second-generation British Indian therapist, registered with the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (MBACP). My approach is integrative, drawing from both Eastern and Western perspectives to offer therapy that’s compassionate, authentic, and inclusive. I believe in meeting you where you are, honouring your story, and supporting you with warmth and respect.

Who I Work With

I welcome adults and young people from all walks of life. My practice is especially supportive of:

  • Neurodivergent clients
  • LGBTQIA+ clients
  • Trauma survivors
  • Young adults in transition
  • Clients from diverse cultures and backgrounds

Whoever you are, you are welcome here. No one is “too much” or “too different”.

Issues I Help With

You might be navigating trauma, anxiety, grief, depression, identity questions, relationship challenges, or life transitions. Whatever you’re facing, I offer a gentle, non-judgemental space to explore what matters to you.

My Approach

Integrative therapy means I draw from different therapeutic traditions, including psychodynamic and transpersonal approaches, to tailor our work to your unique needs. My practice is trauma-informed, which means I prioritise your safety, offer choice, and move at a pace that feels right for you. Together, we’ll create a space where you can feel seen, heard, and supported.

Ready to Talk?

I offer a free initial phone consultation (no obligation) so you can get a sense of how I work. Sessions are available in Hackney (E8), Palmers Green (N13), or online. Whenever you’re ready, I’m here to listen.
